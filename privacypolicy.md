# Privacy Policy for Clowner

**Effective Date:** May 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Clowner (“the Bot”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users interact with the Bot through the Discord platform.

By using Clowner, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

To provide functionality, the Bot may access or process certain information made available through the Discord API, including:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Channel IDs
* Message metadata
* Message content necessary for bot functionality
* Reaction data
* Usage and interaction logs

The Bot only accesses information reasonably necessary to operate its features.

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing

Information collected by the Bot may be used for the following purposes:

* Responding to and reacting to messages
* Providing bot functionality and automation
* Preventing abuse, spam, or malicious usage
* Diagnosing technical issues and improving stability
* Maintaining service security and integrity

We do not use collected information for advertising or profiling purposes.

---

## 4. Message Content and Storage

Clowner is designed to randomly react to messages sent within servers where it is installed.

Message content is generally processed temporarily in memory and is not permanently stored unless required for:

* debugging,
* moderation investigations,
* abuse prevention,
* or system diagnostics.

Temporary logs may be retained for a limited period when necessary for operational purposes.

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Information may only be disclosed:

* when required by applicable law,
* to comply with legal obligations,
* to protect the security or integrity of the service,
* or to trusted infrastructure providers necessary to operate the Bot.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

The Bot relies on third-party services, including but not limited to:

* Discord and the Discord API,

* analytics or monitoring services.

Use of those services is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

* operate the Bot,
* maintain security,

Data that is no longer required is deleted or anonymized where practical.

## 8. Security

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
